circRNAs-SNPs/mutations课题现阶段总结

Latest Date：20190121

circRNAs-SNPs/mutations研究意义（证据支持）

因为在研究一个课题，总要有个研究意义，比如像miRNA-SNP文章中所述：miRNA-related SNPs including SNPs in miRNA genes and targetsites may function as regulatory SNPs through modifying miRNA regulation to affect the phenotypes and disease susceptibility [Ryan et al., 2010]；a number of studies have demonstrated that SNPs in target sites or miRNA genes are associated with diseases [Jazdzewski et al., 2008; Mencia et al., 2009; Ryan et al., 2010; Saunders et al., 2007; Sethupathy and Collins, 2008; Sun et al., 2009]。 再比如lncRNA-SNP文章中所述：Recently, SNPs in lncRNAs were found to be linked to their abnormal expressions and dysregulations, thus play important roles in disease association [Zhang,X et al., 2014]。

所以需要找到和circRNA相关的SNP/mutation或者是circRNA上的SNP/mutation产生影响（尤其在疾病中）的证据，特别是实验证据！

文献证据支持

但检索（“mutation in circRNA” / “snp in circRNA” in WOS、PubMed、Google）结果显示相关研究偏少，现列举如下。

实验证据

  • Ashwal-Fluss, Reut, et al. "circRNA biogenesis competes with pre-mRNA splicing." Molecular cell 56.1 (2014): 55-66. 文章中提到：Disruptive mutations of the circRNA exon splice site by a GU to CA mutation strongly diminish the generation of circRNAs from the minigene (n = 3).（Fly）（影响circRNA形成）
  • Okholm, Trine Line Hauge, et al. "Circular RNA expression is abundant and correlated to aggressiveness in early-stage bladder cancer." NPJ genomic medicine 2.1 (2017): 36. 文章中提到：进行了Mutation analysis of abundant circRNAs，想要知道膀胱癌不同阶段风险和circRNA表达异常是否是由于突变造成的。虽然最后结果为不显著，但是确实观察到circRNAs包含了比预期要多的突变。（但其中有很多值得借鉴的地方，如数据及获取途径）
  • Piwecka, Monika, et al. "Loss of a mammalian circular RNA locus causes miRNA deregulation and affects brain function." Science 357.6357 (2017): eaam8526.（通过knockout）（导致miRNA失调；影响大脑功能）
  • Ma, Han-Bo, et al. "Extensive profiling of circular RNAs and the potential regulatory role of circRNA-000284 in cell proliferation and invasion of cervical cancer via sponging miR-506." American journal of translational research 10.2 (2018): 592.文章中提到： knockdown of circRNA-000284 suppressed cell proliferation and invasion, and caused G0/G1 phase cell cycle arrest. （抑制细胞增殖和侵袭；造成G0/G1期细胞循环停滞）
  • Hsiao, Kuei-Yang, et al. "Non-coding effects of circular RNA CCDC66 promote colon cancer growth and metastasis." Cancer research (2017): canres-1883.文章中提到：knockdown of circCCDC66 inhibited tumor growth and cancer invasion in xenograft and orthotopic mouse models, respectively. highlight a novel oncogenic function of circRNA in cancer progression and metastasis. （抑制肿瘤生长和癌症的侵袭）
  • Zhang, Yang, et al. "The biogenesis of nascent circular RNAs." Cell reports 15.3 (2016): 611-624.文章中提到：Loss of intronic RNA pairing across the circle-forming exons in GCN1L1 completely blocks circGCN1L1 formation. （影响circRNA形成）
  • Burd, Christin E., et al. "Expression of linear and novel circular forms of an INK4/ARF-associated non-coding RNA correlates with atherosclerosis risk." PLoS genetics 6.12 (2010): e1001233. 文章中提到：Expression of cANRIL and proximal ANRIL species correlate with INK4/ARF expression and ASVD–associated SNP genotype. （ASVD-动脉硬化）
  • Holdt, Lesca M., Alexander Kohlmaier, and Daniel Teupser. "Molecular functions and specific roles of circRNAs in the cardiovascular system." Non-coding RNA research (2018). 文章Table 2、3中列举的circRNAs在体内作用，有部分circRNA的功能涉及到circRNA相关的SNP、mutation、knockdown对某疾病产生影响。

非实验证据

  • Circ2Traits数据库依据和疾病相关的SNPs映射到circRNAs序列上建立的circRNA-disease数据库。所以其中涉及到经过一系列分析研究来揭示，circRNA和SNPs之间的关联和广泛疾病都相关。
  • Guo, Wenzhi, et al. "Polymorphisms and expression pattern of circular RNA circ-ITCH contributes to the carcinogenesis of hepatocellular carcinoma." Oncotarget 8.29 (2017): 48169. 文章中提到：SNPs in the circ-ITCH gene region may conclusively affect individual variation in inhibitory effect of circ-ITCH, and modulate individual cancer susceptibility. So investigation of SNPs in circ-ITCH gene contributes to uncovering pathogenesis of HCC.
  • Thomas, Laurent F., and Pål Sætrom. "Circular RNAs are depleted of polymorphisms at microRNA binding sites." Bioinformatics 30.16 (2014): 2243-2246. 文章中提到：The strong selection against SNPs at splice sites likely reflects that splicing is essential in circRNA maturation. （影响circRNA形成）

结论

虽然相关证据偏少，但足以支撑。
